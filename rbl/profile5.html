    About Me

    Experience

    I am Rajkumar, a magician from Delhi, and I have been enchanting audiences with my traditional Indian magic for over two decades. My journey began at a young age, inspired by the rich cultural heritage of India, and I quickly became known for my unique blend of classical Indian magic and modern illusions. I have had the privilege of performing at high-profile events like the Delhi Cultural Fest and various corporate shows, earning accolades such as the Magician of the Year and the Cultural Ambassador Award. My international performances have allowed me to showcase Indian magic on a global stage. Committed to preserving this ancient art, I also conduct workshops for underprivileged children, sharing my passion and skills with the next generation.
